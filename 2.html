Welcome to Bel Air

Bel Air is the most affluent town in the states.
Bel Air (or Bel-Air) is a neighborhood on the Westside of Los Angeles, California, in the foothills of the Santa Monica Mountains.
Founded in 1923, it is the home of The Hannah Carter Japanese Garden and the American Jewish University.
